 # Privacy Policy — LendMate

  Last updated: June 2026

  ## What we collect
  - **Account information**: name, email address, profile photo (via Google Sign-In)
  - **Loan data**: loan amounts, interest rates, EMI schedules you create
  - **Payment proofs**: screenshots you voluntarily upload
  - **Contacts**: only if you grant permission, to select a borrower
  - **Device token**: for push notifications (EMI reminders)

  ## How we use it
  All data is used solely to provide the loan tracking service between you
  and the other party on a loan. We do not sell or share your data with
  third parties for advertising.

  ## Data storage
  Your data is stored securely on Supabase (supabase.com). Payment proof
  images are stored in Supabase Storage. Push notification tokens are
  managed via Firebase (firebase.google.com).

  ## Third-party services
  - Google Sign-In (authentication)
  - Supabase (database and storage)
  - Firebase (push notifications and remote config)
  
  ## Data deletion
  You can delete your account and all associated data from the app's
  Profile screen → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your
  loans, payments, and profile data.
